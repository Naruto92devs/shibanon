    About

    ShibAnon

    ShibAnon ($SHIBANON) is a privacy-focused token that operates on the Ethereum Blockchain. ShibAnon represents decentralization and privacy, with this as our focus we’ve created a transfer mixer to provide anonymity to users across blockchains, starting with Ethereum then expanding to multiple Blockchains.

    What is a

    Transfer

    Mixer?

    Our Transfer Mixer ultimately breaks the informational link between the source wallet and destination wallet.

    Transactions are sent through our validation / privacy process giving you the anonymity you deserve.
